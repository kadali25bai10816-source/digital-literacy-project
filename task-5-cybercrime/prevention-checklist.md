# Checklist for College Students (India)

## **Safety Tips:**

* Use strong and unique passwords for every account (email, Instagram, college portals, etc.)  
* Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for Gmail, social media, and banking apps.  
* Do not click on unknown links sent through WhatsApp, Telegram, or email.  
* Never share your OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone online.  
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email.  
* Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or App Store.  
* Check privacy settings on your social media accounts regularly.  
* Do not share your personal photos, ID cards, or college documents with unknown people online. 


## **UPI / Financial Safety Tips:**

* Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money (PIN is only needed when sending money).  
* Do not scan QR codes sent by strangers or accept unknown payment requests.  
* Always double-check the name before sending money through UPI apps.  
* Do not share screenshots of payment confirmations publicly.

## **Where to Report Cybercrime in India:**

* Report online fraud immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal  
* You can also call the **Cyber Crime Helpline – 1930** to report financial fraud quickly.
